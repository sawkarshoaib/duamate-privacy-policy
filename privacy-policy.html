Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters. DuaMate is designed to provide authentic Islamic Duas while respecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.

1. Data Collection

DuaMate does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No registration, account creation, or login is required to use the app.

2. Local Storage

Favorites, settings, notes, and reminders are stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers.

3. Audio Features

Audio recitation and text-to-speech functionality operate directly on your device. No audio data is collected or uploaded.

4. Notifications

Reminder notifications are generated locally on your device and are not managed through remote servers.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information at risk. All information remains on your device.
